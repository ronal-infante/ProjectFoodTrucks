#projectfoodtruck

Project Food Truck is an educational and networking tool for Houston-based small businesses looking to run a food truck.

The goal is to share advice and create connections among established entrepeneurs and newcomers in Houston, TX.

Ronal Infante is a graduate student at the University of Houston. He is the main contact for the project.
